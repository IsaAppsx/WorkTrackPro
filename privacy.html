Privacy Policy – WorkTrak Pro

Effective Date: February 22, 2026

At WorkTrak Pro, your privacy is our priority.

1. Information We Collect

WorkTrak Pro does not collect any personal information. All your data—tasks, income, expenses, and reports—are stored locally on your device.

2. How Your Data is Used

Your data is used only to provide the app’s features:

  • Tracking tasks
  • Calculating income
  • Generating reports
  • Importing and exporting data

3. Data Sharing

WorkTrak Pro does not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third party. All data remains on your device unless you choose to export it manually.

4. Security

Your data is stored securely in SQLite on your device. Standard security practices are applied to prevent data loss or corruption.

5. Your Rights

You can access, edit, delete, or export your data at any time directly within the app.

6. Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this policy will appear in the app and take effect immediately.
